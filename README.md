# The-Unknown-Maze
Find the center of the mazes to escape! There are 5 levels and your items that help you will be removed after you finish one. Made for Ludam Dare 45. 

Controls:

W - Move forward

A - Turn Left

S - Move back

D - Turn Right

Q - Sidestep left

E - Sidestep right

Shift + S - Quick turnaround
